About Our School

Demo HMS ACADEMY follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which focuses on comprehensive academic growth from Nursery to Class 10. Our goal is to provide quality education that not only prepares students for academic excellence but also fosters personal and social development.

Academic Structure

Nursery to Class 5

A foundation in basic subjects like English, Mathematics, Environmental Science, and Hindi. Emphasis on creative learning through fun activities, storytelling, and hands-on experiences. Interactive learning environments that help young learners develop cognitive, motor, and social skills.
Class 6 to 8
Introduction to core subjects like Science, Social Studies, and Mathematics in greater depth. Regular assessments and projects to help students build strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Co-curricular activities integrated into academics for holistic development.

Class 9 to 10

Rigorous academic curriculum to prepare students for the CBSE Board exams. Focus on core subjects: English, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), and Social Science. Career guidance and extra support classes for students needing help with specific subjects.

Facilities at HMS ACADEMY

At HMS ACADEMY, we are committed to providing world-class facilities to ensure an enriching and well-rounded learning experience for all our students from Nursery to Class 10. Our infrastructure is designed to foster academic, creative, and physical development, offering an environment where students can thrive.

1. Computer Lab

MODERN TECHNOLOGY: Our computer lab is equipped with high-speed internet and modern computers with updated software to keep students up-to-date with the latest technological advancements.
PROGRAMMING & CODING: Students are introduced to basic coding, programming languages, and other IT skills, ensuring digital literacy from an early age.
Interactive Learning: Various software and tools are used for interactive learning and problem-solving.
2. Medical Room
FIRST-AID & CARE: A dedicated medical room is available with trained staff to provide first-aid and basic medical care in case of emergencies.
HEALTH CHECKUPS: Regular health check-ups and vaccination drives are conducted to ensure the well-being of students.

3. Transport Facility

SAFE & RELIABLE: The school provides a fleet of buses equipped with GPS tracking and trained drivers to ensure safe transport for students.
ROUTES: Buses cover all major routes, ensuring convenient pick-up and drop-off points for students.
4. Security
CCTV SURVEILLANCE: The entire school premises are monitored through CCTV cameras to ensure safety and security.
TRAINED GUARDS: Security personnel are stationed at all entry and exit points to oversee student safety at all times.

5. Counseling Services

STUDENT SUPPORT: The school has a team of trained counselors who provide academic, emotional, and career guidance to students, ensuring their overall mental well-being and growth. At HMS ACADEMY , we strive to create a supportive and stimulating environment where students can explore their full potential, both academically and personally.
